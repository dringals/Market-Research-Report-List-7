<p><p>Enhanced Vision Systems (EVS) improve pilot visibility in various conditions by using cameras and sensors to provide real-time images of the surroundings. The market consists of two primary types: Stand-Alone EVS, which operates independently to enhance situational awareness, and Combined Vision Systems (CVS), integrating both EVS and Synthetic Vision Systems (SVS) for a more comprehensive view. CVS offers pilots enhanced data overlays, combining real-world imagery with synthetic data for improved navigation and safety during critical flight phases.</p></p>
