<p><ul><li>Electric Piezometers</li><li>Hydraulic Piezometers</li><li>Pneumatic Piezometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable piezometer market comprises three main types: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic piezometers. Electric piezometers utilize sensors to measure water pressure and provide precise data, making them ideal for real-time monitoring. Hydraulic piezometers rely on the movement of fluid through a manometer to gauge pressure, suitable for various applications. Pneumatic piezometers use air pressure to determine water levels, offering versatility in challenging environments. Each type serves specific needs in environmental monitoring, construction, and engineering projects.</p></p>
