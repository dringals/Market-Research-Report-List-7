<p><p>The subsea leak detection system market includes two primary types: active and passive systems. Active subsea leak detection systems utilize sensors and technologies that actively monitor and identify leaks in real-time, often incorporating mechanisms to provide immediate alerts or responses. Conversely, passive subsea leak detection systems rely on methods that detect changes in the environment or physical characteristics, requiring no active monitoring, and are often used for long-term surveillance of subsea infrastructures. Both play crucial roles in ensuring safety and environmental protection in underwater operations.</p></p>
