<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiation Therapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiation Therapy</li><li>Internal Radiation Therapy</li><li>Systemic Radiation Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation therapy equipment market comprises three main types. External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT) utilizes machines like linear accelerators to administer targeted radiation from outside the body, treating tumors effectively. Internal Radiation Therapy, or brachytherapy, involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near tumors, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Systemic Radiation Therapy involves administering radioactive substances through the bloodstream, allowing targeted treatment of metastasized cancers. Each type plays a crucial role in personalized cancer treatment approaches.</p></p>
