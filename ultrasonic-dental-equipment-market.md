<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Dental Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Type</li><li>Piezoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic dental equipment market is segmented into two main types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric devices. Magnetostrictive devices utilize magnetic fields to produce ultrasonic vibrations, allowing for efficient calculus removal and periodontal therapy. They are renowned for their ability to operate with various tips. In contrast, piezoelectric devices rely on electrical energy to generate vibrations, offering precise control and enhanced effectiveness on hard tissues. Both types are essential in modern dentistry for improving patient outcomes and enhancing procedural efficiency.</p></p>
