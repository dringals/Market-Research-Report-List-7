Airways involve rapid transport via aircraft, ideal for perishable goods needing swift delivery over long distances. Roadways utilize refrigerated trucks for flexible, regional distribution, ensuring freshness from farms to stores. Seaways provide cost-effective shipping of bulk items across oceans, using refrigerated containers to preserve quality during longer transit times. Each mode is crucial for efficient supply chain management in the food industry.</p></p>
