<p><p>The Clot Management Devices market encompasses various technologies designed to treat thromboembolic conditions. Neurovascular embolectomy devices target cerebral clots, while embolectomy balloon catheters facilitate the removal of blockages in blood vessels. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices mechanically extract clots from veins or arteries. Catheter-directed thrombolysis devices use targeted medication to dissolve clots, and inferior vena cava filters (IVCFs) prevent emboli from traveling to the lungs, playing a crucial role in managing venous thromboembolism and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
