<p><p>The microinjection pump market comprises two main types: single injection pumps and dual injection pumps. Single injection pumps are designed for delivering a single precise dose of fluid, ideal for applications requiring consistency in drug delivery. In contrast, dual injection pumps can administer two different substances simultaneously, allowing for more complex therapeutic regimens or research applications. Both types are crucial in biomedical research, drug development, and clinical settings, enhancing precision and control in fluid delivery systems.</p></p>
