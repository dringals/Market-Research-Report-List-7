<p><p>The preclinical imaging systems market encompasses several advanced imaging modalities essential for biomedical research. MRI provides high-resolution soft tissue imaging without ionizing radiation, while PET offers functional imaging through tracer detection, enabling metabolic activity visualization. SPECT combines gamma-ray detection to evaluate tissue function and biology. Micro-CT delivers exceptional x-ray imaging at a microscopic scale for structural analysis. Optical imaging utilizes light to visualize biological processes, and Magnetic Particle Imaging delivers real-time imaging using magnetic nanoparticles, enhancing precision in preclinical studies.</p></p>
