<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retail management software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates on remote servers, allowing retailers to access inventory, sales, and customer data from anywhere with an internet connection. It typically offers flexibility and lower initial costs. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed directly on a retailer's hardware, providing greater control over data security and performance. This option often requires higher upfront investment but may be more suited to larger retailers with specific needs.</p></p>
