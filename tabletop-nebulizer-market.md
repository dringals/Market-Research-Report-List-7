<p><p>The tabletop nebulizer market is primarily divided into two types: compressor nebulizers and non-compressor nebulizers. Compressor nebulizers use a pump to generate a stream of air, converting liquid medication into aerosol for inhalation, making them effective for various respiratory conditions. Non-compressor nebulizers, such as ultrasonic and mesh nebulizers, utilize different technologies to create aerosols without the need for a traditional compressor, offering advantages like quieter operation and portability, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
