<p><p>The well test market comprises several types, including Real Time Well Testing, which provides immediate data analysis to optimize production. Downhole Well Testing focuses on collecting data directly from the wellbore to assess reservoir properties. Reservoir Sampling involves extracting fluid samples to analyze reservoir characteristics and fluid behavior. Surface Well Testing encompasses the evaluation of produced fluids at the surface, monitoring flow rates and pressures to assess overall well performance. Each type plays a crucial role in effective resource management and production optimization.</p></p>
